# ODU Engineering

> Engineering case studies documenting the systems I've designed, built, and continue to improve.

ODU Engineering is where I document the engineering behind the software I build.

Rather than being a traditional developer portfolio, this platform serves as a collection of in-depth engineering case studies that explore the complete lifecycle of real-world software projects—from understanding the problem to designing the architecture, implementing the solution, deploying to production, and reflecting on the lessons learned.

My goal is to showcase not only **what** I've built, but also **how I think**, **how I solve problems**, and **the engineering decisions that shape every system I work on**.

---

## Why I Built ODU Engineering

Most portfolios focus on finished products, screenshots, and a list of technologies.

I wanted something more meaningful.

ODU Engineering was created to document the engineering process behind my work. Every project is presented as a case study, covering the business context, architectural decisions, technical trade-offs, implementation details, challenges, and outcomes.

Whether it's a government platform, a full-stack web application, or an AI-powered system, each case study is written to explain the reasoning behind the solution—not just the final result.

---

Each engineering case study is designed to provide a comprehensive view of how the project was built, including:

- Digital document management
- Production deployment
- Modern citizen-focused user experience

This project serves as the flagship case study for ODU Engineering.
