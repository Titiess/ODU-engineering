Manual ticketing processes often cause bottlenecks, transaction tracking errors, and high operational overhead. For the client, maintaining manual list registers and collecting payment confirmations through direct bank screenshots was highly error-prone and vulnerable to fraud.

The core challenge was to build a system that:
1. Validates checkout requests and payment checkouts securely.
2. Prevents double-spending and order duplication.
3. Automatically issues printable tickets to registered users upon payment completion.
