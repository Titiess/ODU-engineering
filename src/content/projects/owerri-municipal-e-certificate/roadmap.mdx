## What's Next

The platform is in production and serving citizens, but there are clear opportunities for improvement:

- **Multi-Factor Authentication** — adding an additional layer of security for citizen accounts
- **Notification System** — SMS and email notifications for application status changes
- **Audit Logging** — comprehensive audit trails for regulatory compliance and operational visibility
- **Advanced Analytics** — deeper insights into application patterns, processing bottlenecks, and citizen satisfaction
- **AI Document Validation** — automated verification of uploaded documents to reduce manual review burden
- **Multi-Council Expansion** — adapting the platform to serve additional local government councils across Nigeria
- **Mobile Application** — native mobile experience with push notifications for real-time updates
- **Batch Operations** — administrative tools for processing bulk applications during peak periods
