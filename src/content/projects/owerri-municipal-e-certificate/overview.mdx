The Owerri Municipal Council E-Certificate Platform digitises the entire government certificate workflow — from citizen application to verified digital document.

The traditional Certificate of Origin process in Owerri relied on manual paperwork, physical office visits, lengthy processing times, and difficult verification. Citizens often waited weeks to receive certificates, faced opaque processes, and had no reliable way to verify certificate authenticity.

This platform replaces the entire manual workflow with a secure, accessible, and transparent digital service. Citizens can now apply online, upload supporting documents, pay processing fees through Flutterwave, and receive digitally signed certificates — all without visiting a government office.

The system serves the administrative needs of Owerri Municipal Council while providing citizens with a modern, trustworthy government service.
