Deanslist Global is a DAO community engagement platform designed to facilitate decentralized governance through proposal feeds, voting interfaces, and community analytics. Built as a professional collaboration with the Deanslist DAO, the platform enables members to participate in governance decisions directly from their browsers.

The application uses Next.js App Router for server-rendered proposal data, Solana wallet adapter for multi-provider authentication, and Zustand for managing complex client-side state across governance workflows.
